Not a personal account (the-vessel.md does that) but a synthesis of what the archive now holds: what has been confirmed, what is still being mapped, where the practice stands after the dietary clearing, and what the Ayurvedic and astronomical frames add to the read of the earlier data. `women-of-alchemy.md` is structurally unusual: it has a full table of contents with six parts and covers 5,000 years of lineage in depth. For a reader who is new to the archive, encountering this as the second document (after manifesto) is a significant commitment before they've understood what the practice is.

This is not an urgent fix, but it's probably the orientation chain's remaining structural tension after everything else that was addressed this session.
