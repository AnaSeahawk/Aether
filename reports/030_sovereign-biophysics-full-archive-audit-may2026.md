A few things that aren't gaps but are worth naming:

**The agni.md / vilepi-and-the-krama.md relationship.** Agni.md introduces the fire principle but doesn't fully deliver the four agni states (sama/vishama/tikshna/manda). Vilepi-and-the-krama.md actually develops this more. Worth either deepening agni.md or adding a cross-reference so readers know where to go.

**The panchagavya line is underdeveloped.** The five sacred products of the cow (milk, ghee, yogurt, urine, dung) and how they map to the practice is mentioned in the-distillery-in-the-field.md but not fully built out. This is a distinct traditional knowledge base that could have its own piece — or at minimum a deeper section in the-distillery-in-the-field.md. It's also a natural bridge to Indian readers.

**The Inanna bridge.** The two Inanna pieces cover myth and astronomy separately, but the bridge between them — how the Venus astronomy encodes the alchemical process — is implicit rather than explicit. A short connective tissue paragraph in each would help readers see what the two pieces are doing together.

**The presentation is V3 but solo.** The living-waters presentation (50-presentations/) is a 60-minute talk outline. As the archive grows and the experimental record deepens, this will want updating. It currently precedes the two new orientation pieces and the refining-the-source update, so it doesn't yet reflect the "all the practice is for the medicine" framing or the HYP Chapter 3 argument.

**The report on what the body is showing.** There is no current-state document specifically tracking how Ana's body is responding to drinking the distilled fractions daily. The synthesis documents track topical outcomes over three years. But the internal dosing protocol is newer and the observations from that specifically aren't yet documented separately. This will become important as the months accumulate.
