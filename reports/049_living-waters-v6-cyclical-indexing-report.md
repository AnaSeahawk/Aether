## Current State

The Living Waters presentation now stands as Draft V6.

The revision adds an explicit methodological layer: the archive is indexed by position within living cycles, not by calendar date alone.

The new frame is:

- A conventional date locates an event in linear chronology.
- Solar and lunar markers locate the observation within recurring cycles.
- The jar label functions as a field coordinate, not as decoration.
